The Symbiosis Gathering has been my favorite festival for a decade and one that holds a special place in my heart.

A decade ago, my mind was blown/inspirired/expanded in the presence of such an overflowing of creativity; it was - and continues to be - a true inspiration that actively demonstrates such a next-level beauty in music, art, design, tribe, dance and myriad other aspects.
